👉 A fully interactive AR mirror experience in browser

👟 PRODUCT MAPPING SYSTEM
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Map products like this:

Shoes
Attach to foot landmarks
Auto-scale based on foot size estimation
Support walking motion stability
Bags
Shoulder bags → attach to shoulder + torso anchor
Handbags → attach to hand tracking point
Crossbody → dynamic strap simulation
Accessories
Glasses → face landmarks (eyes + nose bridge)
Watches → wrist tracking
Rings/bracelets → finger joints
Necklaces → neck spline tracking
